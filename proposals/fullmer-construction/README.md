## How to turn this into a PDF

1. Open a new Google Doc. Page size: US Letter. Margins: 0.75" top/bottom, 1" left/right.
2. Paste the contents of `workshop-proposal.md` into the doc. Markdown headings (`#`, `##`, `###`) convert to Google Docs heading styles automatically on paste if you have markdown import enabled. Otherwise, set them manually: `#` becomes Title or Heading 1, `##` becomes Heading 1 or 2, `###` becomes Heading 3.
3. At each `<!-- Page N -->` comment, delete the comment and insert a hard page break (`Insert > Break > Page break`).
4. Insert the logo at the placeholder on page 1.
5. Apply brand colors to headings. Apply brand fonts to all text.
6. Replace every `{{PLACEHOLDER}}` and bracketed prompt.
7. Read it out loud one time, all five pages, as if you were speaking to Paige across a coffee table. Any line that sounds like a stage speech, rewrite it.
8. `File > Download > PDF Document (.pdf)`. Save as `fullmer-ai-workshop-proposal.pdf`.
9. Attach the PDF to the email in `email-to-corina.md` and send.

## Pre-send checklist

Anti-slop pass (everything below should be true):

- [ ] Zero em dashes (`—`) or double hyphens (`--`) in the PDF or email body
- [ ] Zero ellipses (`…` or `...`)
- [ ] Zero "not X, it's Y" antithetical constructions
- [ ] Zero banned phrases: "I hope this email finds you well," "I wanted to reach out," "leverage," "seamlessly," "world-class," "passionate about," "tailored approach," "best regards," "in today's [anything] landscape"
- [ ] Restricted words capped at 2 each across the whole piece: enhance, optimize, leverage, utilize, capability, holistic, robust, streamlined, comprehensive
- [ ] All "you" references are singular (to Paige in the PDF, to Corina in the email)
- [ ] One CTA in the PDF (reply with windows). One CTA in the email (reply with windows). No competing actions.
- [ ] Numbers are grounded (the $20,800/$208,000 estimator math, the 25-minute to 8-minute RFI example). If you change them, keep them defensible.
- [ ] Vic's bio is filled in and reads like Vic could have said it himself.

## Voice rules applied

This proposal was written against `/home/user/deskwolf-copywriting-skill/copywriting_guidelines.md` and `SKILL.md`. Specifically:

- **FBA-led opening:** the opportunity section leads with Paige's status shift (Fullmer ahead of every other construction firm in the region on AI fluency), not with workshop features.
